About Me

I am a researcher and lecturer in the Department of Computer Science, Shantou University. I received my PhD degree in Software Engineering from South China University of Technology in 2020, advised by Professor Xiaowei Yang. I am interested in statistical machine learning and probabilistic modeling. My research philosophy is simple and straightfoward: problem --> assumption --> hypothesis space --> loss function --> optimization --> inference. Currently, my research involves designing statistical learning algorithms to solve the problems of domain adaptation and domain generalization.
